Parity Violation from Home Conference

November 2025

"Online cosmology conference built around discussion. This format, pioneered by Cosmology from Home, produces a dynamic and collaborative workshop experience within the online domain. The conference will bring together cosmologists from around the world to discuss parity violation in the cosmic microwave background, baryogenesis, large scale structure, gravitational waves and theory."

V Joint ICTP-Trieste/ICTP-Saifr School on Cosmology

Campus of IFT-UNESP - Sao Paulo, Brazil, July-August 2025

"School aimed at providing students and young researchers with the necessary tools for understanding the current issues in modern cosmology and to familiarize them with how recent observations can be used to constrain different cosmological models and parameters". Lecturs:

  • LSS theory: Roman Scoccimarro
  • Physics of Inflation: Gonzalo Palma
  • A premiere on Gravitational Wave Cosmology: Jose M. Ezquiaga
  • Phyisics of the CMB: Emmanuel Schaan
  • LSS Observations: David Alonso
  • Machine Learning Methods for Cosmology: Francisco Villaescusa
